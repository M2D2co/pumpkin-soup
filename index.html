Pumpkin Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Pumpkin
  • 3 Potatoes
  • 1 Sweet Potato
  • 3 Carrots
  • 4 Cups of Broth
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

Peal and cube the pumpkin, sweet potato, potatoes, and carrots.

Put them in a pot and add enough broth to cover the vegetables.

Add salt and pepper to taste.

Bring to boil. Once boiling, reduce heat and let simmer until the vegetables are tender (about 30 minutes).

Once vegetables are tender, remove from heat and blend the vegetables and broth together.

Serve with sour cream or plain greek yogurt.
